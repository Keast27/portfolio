About

Hi! My name is Kayleigh East and I'm a Game Design and Development major at RIT! To me, games are a form of expression. There’s a lot of love that goes into each character, move set, and line of code. It’s a bunch of creatives working together to portray a feeling and a story to someone, and I love being a part of that process. When I'm not making some cool games, I'm usually either drawing or making puns. I'm a flexible coder and quick learner who's worked on multiple programming projects in different languages.
